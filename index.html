Working to promote transparency and empower citizens through data.

OpenLexington is a non-profit, non-partisan organization that is working to promote local transparency and empower citizens through open data. We are working with the Lexington Fayette Urban County Government to make public data more open, available and useful. OpenLexington Volunteers are composed of citizen engineers, professional software developers, analysts, artists, designers, students, and concerned residents from many different backgrounds. However, the singular constant that binds us is our passion and the desire to make a positive change in Lexington.

Bringing open government to Lexington through the grass roots initiative OpenLexington in partnerships with national campaigns like Public=Online and local organizations. Sign the pledge to help make public data readily available online.
